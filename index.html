About IBP Vridhaashram

IBP Vridhaashram provides comprehensive care for elderly residents, focusing on comfort, health, and companionship.

Regular activities such as storytelling, yoga, games, music, and cultural events keep our residents active and happy.

Safe, clean, and comfortable facilities with dedicated staff ensure a nurturing environment for elders.

Our Mission

Providing love, care, and companionship to the elderly residents at IBP.

Our Vision

Creating a welcoming environment for elders and volunteers to connect meaningfully.
